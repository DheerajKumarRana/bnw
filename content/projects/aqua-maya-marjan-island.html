It's Where Water Meets Wonder

Aqua Maya’s architecture is a dialogue between nature and design. Rising 2B + G + 9 + R floors high, the tower rests on a landscaped podium that connects every part of the building effortlessly. Its balcony-led façade mimics the soft movements of sea waves, brought to life by subtle illumination at night. The use of earthy tones and an articulated structure gives the building a warm identity, grounded in modern sophistication.
